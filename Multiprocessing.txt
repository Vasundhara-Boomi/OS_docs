MULTIPROCESSING IS A SYSTEM THAT HAS TWO OR MORE THAN ONE PROCESSORS. IN THIS, CPUS ARE ADDED FOR INCREASING THE COMPUTING SPEED OF THE SYSTEM. BECAUSE OF MULTIPROCESSING, THERE ARE MANY PROCESSES THAT ARE EXECUTED SIMULTANEOUSLY. MULTIPROCESSING IS FURTHER CLASSIFIED INTO TWO CATEGORIES:
SYMMETRIC MULTIPROCESSING, ASYMMETRIC MULTIPROCESSING.
